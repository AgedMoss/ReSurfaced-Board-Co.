ReSurfaced Board Co.

Who We Are

It isn't a secret anymore, our oceans are filled with plastic. This was the driving force behind ReSurface Board Co. We wanted to take the mounds of plastic polluting our oceans and repurpose them into surfboards. Now the plastic that was once floating on the surface is being used to ride a barrel.

About Our Company

Our Company Morals

Resurface Board Co takes pride in everyone from all walks of life working for the greater cause. Surfing is for everyone, and we hire those who are as passionate about ocean cleanup as we are. We hire based on passion and the desire to fix the world. We do not discriminate based on race, religion, gender, national origin, sexual orientation or age.

Our Employees

How Our Workers Turn Ideas Into Actions

Our employees are our friends, and we entrust in them to make good decisions at work every day. We pride ourselves on a safe work environment and a realistic working wage. We utilize independent contractors to collect and safely melt down the plastic. Then it is shipped to us to be molded and shaped. After the board is shaped it is sealed with an environmentally friendly epoxy. Then the board is buffed to a clean shine. Our employees are the backbone of this endeavor to clean our oceans. As an Equal Opportunity Employer, we pride ourselves on a diverse workforce and a work environment free of discrimination.

Our Hiring Process

New Team Member Selection

Resurfaced Board Co. has a selection process for how we choose our new team members. We list out all the job requirements and what is involved in a career with us, so there isn't any surprises. Questions in the interviewing process are job related only, so there is no need to feel uncomfortable at any time during the interview. We like to promote those who are passionate about our company, all applications for promotion are considered before making a final decision. References may be contacted when a contingent offer of employment is extended to the candidate prior to start date.

Pre-Employment Testing

What Do We Test?

A pre-employment test or tests may be administered prior to a contingent offer of employment. Positions of operating machines or delivery driver are subject to pre-employment as well as random drug screenings. Positions for office personell is subject to a pre-employment aptitude test pertaining to the job desired. Warehouse employees must be able to lift 50lbs and stand for long periods of time. A physical must be taken following the contingent offer of employment. We pride ourselves on a drug-free work place. Anyone suspected of illicit drug use during work hours may be subject to a random drug test.
